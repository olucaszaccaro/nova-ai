# AI Course — Go-to-Market Strategy

> **North Star:** Build the most accessible AI education brand in Portuguese, using free content to create a loyal audience, then convert them into paying students.

> **Positioning Decision (locked):** Market outcomes, teach Claude. The hook is always "para de brincar com IA — aprende a usar do jeito certo." Claude is the primary tool used throughout — positioned as "the most powerful AI for non-technical people." Other AIs (Suno for music, Midjourney/Ideogram for images) appear only when Claude is the weaker choice. As Claude's brand grows in Brazil, this becomes a durable competitive moat.

> **Tagline (approved):** "Para de brincar com IA. Aprende a usar do jeito certo."
> **Hero headline:** "Você não está usando 5% do potencial da IA. Vem aprender o restante."

## 1. The Model (in one sentence)

Give away so much value for free that people feel stupid NOT paying for the next step.

This is pure Hormozi: the free course is not a teaser — it's a complete, genuine transformation. The paid course is just the next logical chapter.

## 2. Avatar

Be precise. The more specific, the more the content resonates.

**Primary Avatar:** Any Brazilian person, 20–45 years old, who hears about AI everywhere but has no idea where to start. They're not technical. They may be a professional, an entrepreneur, a student, a creator, or just a curious person who wants to use AI beyond the basic chat. They've opened ChatGPT, asked something, and closed it. They have 30–60 minutes a day to learn something if it actually works.

**Expanded scope — NOT just office professionals.** The course speaks to anyone who wants to:
- Create things they couldn't create before (music, sites, images, tools)
- Solve personal and professional problems faster
- Automate repetitive tasks in their life or work
- Actually understand how AI works instead of just scratching the surface

**Their #1 frustration:** "I know AI is powerful but I don't know how to use it properly."
**Their #1 dream:** "I want to be the person who actually gets things done with AI."
**Their objection:** "I'm not a tech person — this isn't for me."
**The answer to that objection:** Lucas isn't a programmer either. He uses AI to create apps, music, websites, and tools — without a line of code.

**Positioning line:**
> "Para de brincar com IA. Aprende a usar do jeito certo."

**Secondary line:**
> "Você não está usando 5% do potencial da IA. Vem aprender o restante."

## 4. Platform Stack & Roles

Each platform has ONE job. Don't mix them.

```
INSTAGRAM (Reels)   →   Discovery & top of funnel
     ↓
LANDING PAGE        →   Lead capture (name + phone only)
     ↓
WHATSAPP CHANNEL    →   Community + course delivery + sales
     ↓
YOUTUBE             →   Depth, SEO, long-term asset
```

### Instagram — Discovery Engine
- **Reels**: 15–60s hooks that solve micro-problems. This is your ad. Post 5–7x/week.
- **Stories**: Daily. Behind the scenes, polls, "did you know," CTAs to link in bio.
- **Posts/Carousels**: Evergreen content, tips, frameworks. Repurpose from YouTube.
- **Bio link**: Always points to the landing page.

### Landing Page — The Funnel Gate
- One page. One CTA. Zero distractions.
- Headline: "Aprenda a usar IA do zero — entre na comunidade gratuita"
- Fields: Name + Phone → WhatsApp group redirect
- No email newsletter. WhatsApp has 90%+ open rate vs ~20% for email in Brazil.

### WhatsApp Channel/Group — The Core
- This is where the relationship lives.
- New member joins → gets the free course link immediately (automated welcome)
- Post 3–4x/week: tips, wins from students, teasers, mini-lessons
- This is also where you sell — warm audience, high trust, no algorithm

### YouTube — Long-Term SEO Asset
- Post 1x/week: 10–20 min tutorials
- Free course modules can live here (unlisted or public)
- Titles optimized for search: "Como usar o ChatGPT para [specific task]"
- YouTube compounds over time — it's the slow-burn engine

## 5. The Free Course — Your Lead Magnet

**Hormozi rule:** The lead magnet is a complete solution to a narrow problem that, once solved, reveals a bigger problem you solve in the paid course.

### Free Course Design

**Name:** Start.ai
**Tagline:** "Para de brincar com IA. Aprende a usar do jeito certo."
**Promise:** "Em 5 dias, você vai descobrir como usar IA de verdade — não como chat, mas como ferramenta para criar, resolver e construir qualquer coisa."

**Format:** 5 short video modules, 10–15 min each. Lives in a Notion hub or YouTube playlist (unlisted).

**Access:** Join WhatsApp group → receive link automatically.

**4 Pillars that anchor the course:**
- 🧠 **Entender** — Como IA realmente funciona (tokens, contexto, modelos, skills, agentes, Claude vs outras IAs)
- ✨ **Criar** — Textos, músicas, imagens, vídeos, sites, ferramentas digitais simples
- 💡 **Resolver** — Problemas do trabalho, da vida pessoal, do dia a dia
- ⚡ **Automatizar** — Tarefas repetitivas, fluxos com IA, seu AI Playbook

**Module structure:**

| Dia | Módulo | Quick Win |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| 1 | **Entender IA de verdade** — tokens, contexto, modelos, por que você está usando errado | Mindset shift — they finally understand what AI actually is |
| 2 | **Criar qualquer coisa** — textos, músicas, imagens, ferramentas simples com Claude | They create something they never thought they could make |
| 3 | **Resolver problemas** — use cases pessoais e profissionais, prompt certo para cada situação | They solve a real problem from their own life in the session |
| 4 | **Automatizar sua rotina** — fluxos repetitivos, construir seu AI Playbook pessoal | They identify 3 things to automate and start one |
| 5 | **Seu sistema de IA** — como integrar tudo, próximos passos, o que vem depois | They leave with a personal AI system and want the next level |

**Key design principle:** Module 5 ends with a cliffhanger. They've had their first wins across all 4 pillars. Now they want the complete system. That's Rise.ai.

**Rules for module content:**
- Primary tool: Claude. Secondary tools (Suno, Midjourney/Ideogram) only when Claude is weaker.
- Examples must span beyond office/work: personal life, creativity, fun, family — not just productivity.
- Never say "criar apps para publicar na App Store." Say "criar ferramentas digitais simples para uso próprio."
- Teach fundamentals (tokens, contexto, skills, agentes) — this is the core differentiator.

## 6. Content Pillars (The 4 Types of Content)

Every piece of content fits one of these four buckets. Balance them.

### Pillar 1 — PAIN (40% of content)
Agitate the problem. Make people feel the urgency.
> "Se você ainda não sabe usar IA, aqui está o que vai acontecer com seu emprego nos próximos 2 anos…"

### Pillar 2 — PROOF (20% of content)
Real results from real people. Screenshot wins. Student stories.
> "Essa aluna economizou 3 horas por semana na primeira semana. Veja o que ela fez."

### Pillar 3 — EDUCATION (30% of content)
Teach something specific and immediately useful. These go viral.
> "3 prompts do ChatGPT que vão mudar como você trabalha [Tutorial]"

### Pillar 4 — VISION (10% of content)
Paint the picture of what's possible. Aspirational. Future identity.
> "Daqui 6 meses, você pode ser a pessoa no trabalho que todo mundo pede ajuda sobre IA."

## 7. Hormozi Content Formula

**For every Reel/Short:**

```
HOOK (0–3 sec): Specific, counterintuitive, or shocking
    → "A maioria das pessoas usa o ChatGPT errado"

RETAIN (3–30 sec): Deliver the promise immediately
    → Show the right way, step by step

REWARD (final 5 sec): Give them the win + soft CTA
    → "Aprenda mais: link na bio"
```

**The give-to-ask ratio: 3.5:1**
For every 4 pieces of content, 3.5 give pure value, 0.5 mention the free course or community. Never feel salesy. When you are genuinely useful, selling is just a natural next step.

**Hormozi's volume rule:** You cannot out-educate your competition. Post more than feels comfortable. The algorithm rewards consistency and the audience rewards generosity.

## 10. Monetization Roadmap (3 Phases)

### Phase 1 — Audience Building (Month 1–3)
**Goal:** 1,000 WhatsApp members, 5,000 Instagram followers
**Actions:**
- Post 5–7 Reels/week
- Launch free course
- 1 YouTube video/week
- Zero selling — only giving

**Key metric:** WhatsApp group growth rate

### Phase 2 — Warming & Proof (Month 3–5)
**Goal:** Collect 20+ testimonials, refine content based on what lands
**Actions:**
- Run "beta testers" through free course and document their wins
- Ask for video testimonials
- Start teasing the paid course ("something big is coming")
- Ramp up Stories engagement (polls, Q&As)

**Key metric:** Story reply rate, DM volume

### Phase 3 — Launch (Month 5–6)
**Goal:** First R$10k–R$50k revenue from founding cohort
**Actions:**
- 5-day live launch event (webinar or WhatsApp live series)
- Day 1: Pain (why you need to learn AI now)
- Day 2: Possibility (what's achievable, student wins)
- Day 3: Method (how the course works)
- Day 4: Objections (it's not for tech people, I don't have time, etc.)
- Day 5: Offer + urgency (founding members close in 48h)

**Key metric:** Revenue, conversion rate from WhatsApp to purchase

> GGLabs = AI for practitioners and researchers

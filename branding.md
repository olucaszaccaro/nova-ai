## Brand Name

**Nova AI**

Why it works:
- "Nova" = new in Portuguese (speaks directly to the avatar — new to AI, new start)
- In English, a nova is a star explosion — sudden, powerful, unavoidable light
- Short, globally understood, bilingual without trying
- The top course shares the academy name = brand is the destination

**Tagline:** The AI school for people, not programmers.

**Content visual style:** Very dark near-black backgrounds (#080D06 / #1A2F0A) with vivid lime green (#96D034) accents. Inspired by BravoPay. High contrast, energetic, immediately thumb-stopping on Instagram. Completely distinct from the blue/purple AI education landscape.

## Course Ladder

### Start.ai — FREE
Your first steps in AI.
5 modules, 10–15 min each. From zero to real results in one week. This is the lead magnet that builds the audience and earns trust.

### Rise.ai — PAID (Core Product)
Build your AI system.
The complete course. Teaches students how to build a personal AI workflow with Claude that saves 2+ hours every day. This is the primary monetization product.

### Nova.ai — PAID (Advanced)
Become the AI expert.
For students who want to go all in — automation, AI agents, business applications. Named after the academy itself: completing Nova.ai = reaching the brand's promise.
